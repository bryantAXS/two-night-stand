What is Two Night Stand

Two Night Stand is a 2-day, 1-night experiment where creative types (designers, bloggers, photographers, coders illustrators, video artists, etc.) converge to tackle a mystery project.

Participants will be selected based on expertise and point of view, with the entire process documented and shared online.
