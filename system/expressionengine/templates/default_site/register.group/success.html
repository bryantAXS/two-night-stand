Event FAQs

What is Two Night Stand?

Two Night Stand is an invite only event that brings together a randomly seclected group of creatives and catalogs the creative process over a two day period.

We consider it a creative growth experience.

How many people are participating?

Collaboration #1 requires 20 creatives; designers, illustrators, photographers, brainstormers and copywriters/bloggers. Participation is invite only. If you are interested in participating, fill out the form to the right and if you are selected to participate you will be notified.

What do I need to bring?

Bring the tools of your trade. Designers, you probably want to bring your laptop, idea people your brain and a sketchbook, photographers a camera, etc. Bring whatever will help you to ideate.

Why is there a cost?

The $25 fee covers food and basic supplies for the event.
